About Us

What is Eco Code?

Eco Code is a 24-hour hackathon bringing together students, developers, and environmental enthusiasts to create innovative solutions for our planet's most pressing challenges.

Whether you're a seasoned developer or writing your first line of code, Eco Code welcomes hackers of all skill levels. Our mission is to harness the power of technology to address climate change, promote sustainability, and protect our natural world.

As an official MLH member event, we uphold the highest standards of inclusivity, learning, and community. Join us in coding for a cause that matters.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

A hackathon is an invention marathon where people come together to build creative solutions to problems. At Eco Code, you'll have 36 hours to create a project focused on environmental sustainability. No prior experience needed!

Eco Code is open to all students and recent graduates (within 1 year). Whether you're a computer science major, environmental studies student, or just curious about tech, you're welcome to join!

Eco Code is completely FREE! We provide meals, snacks, swag, and everything you need to hack. Just bring your laptop and your creativity!

You can register alone or with a team of up to 4 people. If you don't have a team, we'll have team formation activities at the start of the event to help you find the perfect teammates!

Bring your laptop, charger, any hardware you want to use, and a sleeping bag if you plan to rest on-site. We'll provide everything else including power strips, WiFi, food, and lots of caffeine!

Yes! We follow the MLH Code of Conduct. We're dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

Travel reimbursement details are still being finalized. Check back closer to the event or follow our social media for updates!

All work must be done during the hackathon. You can come with ideas and designs, but no code should be written before hacking begins. This ensures a fair competition for everyone!
