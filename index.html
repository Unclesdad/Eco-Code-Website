Challenge Tracks

Choose Your Mission

Teams can choose to submit for one of our five tracks

Climate Action

Create solutions to reduce or mitigate the effects of climate change, such as projects working to lower carbon emissions, improve energy efficiency, or track climate data.

Track Prize TBD

Ecosystem

Utilize technology to better strengthen ecosystems and preserve biodiversity. Projects can focus on monitoring wildlife and habitats, protecting endangered species, and supporting long-term ecological resilience.

Track Prize TBD

Green Education

Design methods to cultivate understanding and action on sustainability among all people, including ways for widespread collaboration or interactive educational experiences like games or simulations.

Track Prize TBD

Waste Reduction

Innovate around waste reduction, commodity-focused tools for reuse or recycling, or zero-waste products. Projects aim to transform communities, resources, and materials to be more clean and eco-friendly.

Track Prize TBD

Sustainability

Explore ways to engage with sustainability in your technologies, algorithms, and hardware. This open track invites ambitious, creative, interdisciplinary solutions of all forms.
